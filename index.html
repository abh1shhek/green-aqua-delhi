Our Story

Home for Planted Aquariums
& aquatic life.

Green Aqua Delhi is the trusted destination for Custom Aquascapes, Aquatic plants and Livestock. Since 2019, we've been helping aquarium hobbyists across India bring their underwater worlds to life.

From lush nature aquascapes to hardy community tanks, we curate healthy livestock and vibrant plants, and share the knowledge to keep them thriving. Whether you're setting up your first nano tank or refining a competition-grade layout, we're here for every step.
